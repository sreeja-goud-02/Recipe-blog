Masala Dosa

Description

A crispy rice crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture, served with chutney and sambar. A South Indian classic loved by many.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind soaked rice and dal into a smooth batter, ferment overnight.
  3. Prepare the potato filling by sautéing mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and onions; mix with mashed potatoes and salt.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter, spread thinly, drizzle oil around edges, and cook until golden and crisp.
  5. Place the potato filling inside the dosa, fold, and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
