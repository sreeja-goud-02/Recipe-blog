Medu Vada

South Indian crispy lentil fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • 1 tbsp chopped curry leaves
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal for 5-6 hours. Drain and grind to a thick, fluffy batter with minimal water.
  2. Add salt, pepper, curry leaves, onion, green chili, and ginger to the batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan. Wet your palm, take a small portion of batter, shape like a donut.
  4. Gently slide it into the hot oil. Fry until golden and crispy on both sides.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar for a perfect traditional combo.
