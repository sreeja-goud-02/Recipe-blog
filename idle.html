Idli Sambar

Soft steamed rice cakes with a hearty lentil stew

Ingredients

For Idli:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

For Sambar:

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Tamarind pulp – 2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Sambar powder – 1.5 tbsp
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

To Make Idli:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter. Mix together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.
  3. Pour the batter into greased idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.

To Make Sambar:

  1. Cook toor dal with turmeric until soft and mash it.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried chilies, and curry leaves.
  3. Add onions, sauté till translucent. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Add sambar powder, tamarind pulp, salt, and cooked dal. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot, fluffy idlis with a bowl of steaming sambar and coconut chutney on the side.
