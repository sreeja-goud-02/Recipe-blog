Rasam Rice

Tangy and spicy tomato-based soup served with rice, flavored with tamarind, garlic, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, chopped
  • 1 lemon-sized tamarind ball soaked in warm water
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1 teaspoon oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Extract tamarind juice by squeezing the soaked tamarind in water and strain the pulp.
  2. In a pot, add tamarind juice, chopped tomato, crushed garlic, turmeric, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  3. Crush black peppercorns and add to the pot. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and dried red chilies. Once they crackle, pour the tempering into the rasam.
  5. Simmer for another 5 minutes and remove from heat.
  6. Mix cooked rice with the rasam and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot as a comforting meal.

Serving Suggestion

Enjoy rasam rice with papad or a simple vegetable stir fry for a complete South Indian meal.
