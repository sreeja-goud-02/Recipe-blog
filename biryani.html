Biryani

A fragrant mixed rice dish with spices, herbs, and marinated meat or vegetables, popular across South India.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or vegetables
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • Whole spices: bay leaves, cinnamon sticks, cloves, cardamom
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a large pan, add whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  6. Add chicken or vegetables, yogurt, and cook until tender.
  7. Mix in coriander, mint leaves, and green chilies. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. In a separate pot, boil water with salt. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the rice.
  9. Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat/vegetables mixture.
  10. Sprinkle garam masala and a few coriander leaves on top.
  11. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat ("dum") for 20-25 minutes.
  12. Turn off heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot biryani with raita, salad, or boiled eggs for an authentic feast.
