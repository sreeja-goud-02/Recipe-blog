Chicken Curry

A classic South Indian chicken curry rich in spices and flavors, perfect with rice or bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 sprigs curry leaves
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a heavy pan. Add cumin seeds and curry leaves, sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook till oil separates.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook on medium heat until they start to brown.
  6. Mix in yogurt and green chilies, stir well, and cover to cook for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Add water if needed to adjust the gravy consistency, simmer until chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice, roti, or paratha.
